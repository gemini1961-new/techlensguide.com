How to use

  • 1. Register & Login: First, register on the Chat Thing website. After successful registration, log in to your account.
  • 2. Create a Conversation: On the home page, click on 'Start Conversation'. This will generate a new chat window where you can start your chat.
  • 3. Utilize AI Features: Understand the different AI features and use them to spice up your chats. You can access these features from the toolbar on the chat screen.
  • 4. Interact & Respond: Engage in the conversation and respond to the generated messages. You will notice how the AI involves itself actively in the conversation, grasping your context, humor, and emotions.
  • 5. Enjoy & Share: Enjoy your creative interaction with the AI chatbot. You can also share the conversation with friends or post them on your social media platforms for others to enjoy.
