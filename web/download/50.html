How to use

  • 1. Download the iStory app from your preferred App Store.
  • 2. Create your account using your email id and choose a snazzy username that'll be your storyteller identity.
  • 3. Now, navigate through different genres or search for specific narratives in the search bar. Found a story that catches your eye? Click on it to dive in and get lost in its spellbinding plot.
  • 4. Want to keep a track of the stories you're reading or plan to read? Use the 'My Library' section to categorize them.
  • 5. Enjoyed a story and want more from the same writer? Click on the author's name to find more narrations from them.
  • 6. If you have a story brewing in your mind, the 'Write' section is just for you! Go ahead, flood the world with your word magic with iStory.
