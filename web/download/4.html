How to use

  • 1. To begin your animated drawing journey, navigate to our Animated Drawings platform and create a new account or sign in to an existing one.
  • 2. Once you are in, find and click on the 'Create' button to start a new project. A canvas will appear where you can start adding your touch.
  • 3. Use the toolbar provided for different commands such as pencil, brush, eraser, fill and much more to design your masterpiece. Make sure you play with the different tools and discover all their wonders!
  • 4. After you have finished sketching, click on the 'Animation' option. Here lies the magic! Start animating your drawings and watch them come to life.
  • 5. Don't forget to save your work frequently in the 'File' menu to avoid losing your precious creations. Happy drawing!
