How to use

  • 1. To get started with IngestAI, sign up on our platform with an active email and create a password that's easy to remember but hard for others to guess.
  • 2. Once registered, navigate to the 'Projects' section where you can initiate a new project. Provide a project name and select the type of AI service you want to implement.
  • 3. After setting up your project, you'll be directed to a dashboard. Here, you can upload your data files, either through Drag and Drop or by clicking 'Upload Files'. Thereafter, select the appropriate AI model for your data.
  • 4. Now, with all the pieces in place, click 'Run AI'. Sit back and watch as IngestAI seamlessly integrates AI into your project.
  • 5. Once the AI has completed its operation on your data, you will be able to view the results, visualize the data and even export the analysis. Remember, with IngestAI, the future is at your fingertips!
