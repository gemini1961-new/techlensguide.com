How to use

  • 1. Begin by creating a new story. You can either enter a blog post URL or an existing script, after which Lumen5's AI will generate a video storyboard.
  • 2. Curate and customize your storyboard. Change the text, media, music, and more to suit your needs. Arrange the scenes and set the pace of your video.
  • 3. Don't forget to make your content sparkle with your brand. Customize backgrounds and colors and add your logo.
  • 4. Once your storyboard looks dazzling, click 'preview' to see your creation. If everything’s just the way you want it, you are ready to publish your video masterpiece.
