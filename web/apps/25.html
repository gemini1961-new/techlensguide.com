How to use

  • 1. Download and launch PhotoRoom on your device. The user-friendly interface loads quickly and presents various options.
  • 2. Select the 'Take Photo' option. You can capture a new photo or choose an existing one from your library.
  • 3. Once the photo is positioned, use the automatic background remover tool to delete unwanted backgrounds or objects.
  • 4. Alternatively, if you want to change background, select 'Change Background'. A variety of backgrounds are available to choose from.
  • 5. Use the 'Add Text' option if you wish to insert a text into your photo. Customize the font and color to your liking.
  • 6. The fun part is the ‘Effects’ tab. Add your desired effects like filters or shapes to enhance your image.
  • 7. Lastly, save your new creation and share it with the world. Exporting your finished product is as simple as clicking 'Save' and choosing your preferred format.
