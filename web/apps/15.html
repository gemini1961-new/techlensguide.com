Unlock the artist in you and dive into a world of creativity with Draw Things! This interactive online platform is designed to challenge and inspire all ages and skill levels. A perfect tool for those wanting to learn or improve their drawing skills. With tons of unique features, including a library of different drawing materials, countless color palettes, and a variety of canvases to choose from, Draw Things promises to make every user’s drawing experience a fun-filled adventure. Whether you're a beginner just starting out, or a seasoned professional, Draw Things invites you to express your imagination, hone your skills, and create your very own masterpieces!

How to use

  • 1. Visit Draw Things website and sign up for a free account. With this account, you'll have access to all of its features, and you can save your creations in your personal gallery.
  • 2. Choose the materials, color palette and the canvas size you want to work with from the library. Remember, experimenting is half the fun!
  • 3. Use the easy-to-navigate tools bar to start drawing. Simple strokes or complex, it’s all up to you!
  • 4. Save your artwork. You can choose to keep it private or share it with the Draw Things community.
  • 5. Go ahead and explore the gallery, get inspired by other users' artworks, and interact with the community. Learning should be fun and social!
